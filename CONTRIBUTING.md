# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in contributing to this project! Please read and adhere to the following guidelines.

## Legal Compliance

All contributions involving AI-generated content must comply with the laws and regulations of your country and jurisdiction. Contributors are responsible for ensuring their submissions do not violate any local, state, or national laws regarding:

- Data privacy and protection
- Copyright and intellectual property rights
- Content regulations and misinformation
- Any other legally regulated areas in your jurisdiction

**Violations of local laws related to AI-generated content may result in legal penalties, including fines, prosecution, or other legal action.**

## Contribution Guidelines

### Before You Start

1. Read and understand the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
2. Ensure your contribution complies with all applicable laws in your country
3. Check that your contribution doesn't duplicate existing work

### How to Contribute

1. Fork the repository
2. Create a new branch for your feature or fix:
   ```bash
   git checkout -b feature/your-feature-name
   ```
3. Make your changes while ensuring legal compliance
4. Commit your changes with clear, descriptive messages
5. Push your branch and submit a pull request

### Pull Request Requirements

- Provide a clear description of your changes
- Reference any related issues
- Ensure your code follows the project's style guidelines
- Test your changes thoroughly
- Include any necessary documentation updates
- Declare that your contribution complies with applicable laws

### Code Standards

- Write clean, readable code
- Include comments for complex logic
- Follow consistent formatting with the rest of the project
- Test your changes before submitting

## Reporting Issues

When reporting issues:
- Include a clear description of the problem
- Provide steps to reproduce the issue
- Include relevant error messages or logs
- Note your environment and country/jurisdiction if relevant to legal compliance

## Questions or Concerns

If you have questions about legal compliance or contribution requirements, please open an issue for discussion before proceeding with your contribution.
